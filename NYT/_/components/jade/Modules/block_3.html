CATCH UP WITH CLIMATE EDITOR, THE NEW YORK TIMES
Hannah Fairfield
In the January 2017 press release introducing Hannah Fairfield as our climate editor, The Times wrote: “No topic is more vital than climate change and covering it requires drive, creativity and more than a little bit of specialized knowledge. We are thrilled to announce that we found an editor with all those qualities — and more — to lead our climate coverage: Hannah Fairfield.” She has been with The Times for nearly 15 years, and holds two master’s degrees from Columbia University, in journalism and environmental science. Just saying. But we’ll let her speak for herself.
