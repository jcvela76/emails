Did you know?
Environmental concerns, and battles over them, have been around for a long time. Today it’s global warming. Some 30 years ago in the U.S., it was acid rain, urban smog and toxic chemicals in the air. Even then, science, industry and politics were among the stakeholders — and combatants. The Times article below captures an early chapter in the ongoing story: the Senate’s approval of a Clean Air Act measure, from April 4, 1990.
