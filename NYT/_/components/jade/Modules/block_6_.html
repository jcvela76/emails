New In The Newspaper: A2/A3
You’ll find addictive new content in the first two inside pages of the main news section. The March 2 debut of this reimagined space included: a selection of arresting facts pulled from articles across that day’s paper; the story behind a Times story; a recommendation of a current Times video and audio; useful tips for daily life; a weekday Mini Crossword, previously available only in digital form; and more. You can’t get there from here, but we hope you’ll check it out the next time you open the newspaper.
